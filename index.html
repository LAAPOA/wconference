Registration Fee Covered by LAAPOA
LAAPOA members are invited to attend the upcoming Leadership & Training Symposium hosted by the Los Angeles Women Police Officers & Associates (LAWPOA). This professional development opportunity allows members to gain leadership insight and network with law enforcement professionals.
LAAPOA will cover the registration fee for participating members. At this time there are 9 spots available.
Please note this is a LAWPOA event and should not be confused with LAAPOA. It is an excellent opportunity to network, learn, and strengthen your ability to advocate for yourself professionally.
